Bachelor Thesis (2022)

Bioinformatics / R
Evaluation of Oxford Nanopore Sequencing data classification beyond canonical nucleotides. Nanopore sequencing is a technology that allows to sequence DNA/RNA sequences. This thesis therefore focuses on a published paper titled ”Native molecule sequencing by nano-ID reveals synthesis and stability of RNA isoforms”, which presents a tool, nano-ID, allowing to detect one specific RNA isoform. This research concentrates on integrity of their data and the reusability of their code. Moreover, it aims to evaluate the performance of the neural network developed in the nano-ID tool, showing that it is possible to detect RNA isoforms.
